## Current Product Decision

The dashboard should be a decision surface first and an evidence browser second. The first screen must answer:

1. What changed?
2. Why does it matter to Algolia?
3. Who should act?
4. Can I trust the evidence?

Raw signals remain available, but they must not be the primary homepage experience.
